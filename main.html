Ascetic Nature

Computer generated image series. read more

Ascetic Nature is a series of 3 still images inspired from nature. What started as an initial idea to recreate grass ended up being an exploration of natural elements with digital art, as playing with colors, shapes and their position brought a whole new perspective.

October 2017

Space Travel

Constellation Generator. read more

Space Travel is a constellation generator which creates constellations while giving the illusion of travelling through space. There are 500-700 stars being drawn on the screen in a 3D space and with each frame the stars travel from the back of the screen towards the front.

December 2017

The Hidden Tears

Photography series of a speculative design object. read more

The Hidden Tears helps women who have experienced miscarriage deal with their loss. Having developed maternal instincts in the short course of their pregnancy the object allows them to deploy these feelings by taking care of a baby.
