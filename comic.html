Life in the UAE

A satire comic

Website

Life in the UAE is a satire representing some of the most common stereotypes that visitors and people from the outside hold about the United Arab Emirates. This project was created in collaboration with Mai Khaled, a UAE national and Navya, a student from India. We shared what stereotypes we have come across about our lives in the UAE from both the perspective of being a local and living in the country as an expat. The comic is meant to address the sometimes-ridiculous images that people draw when imagining the UAE.

Built with: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
